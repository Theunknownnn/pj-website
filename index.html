What Is It About?

Percy Jackson The Chalice Of The Gods is based around a highschool kid who is a demigod trying to get into college but it turns out he needs 3 recommendation letters from 3 different Gods.

Whats The Plot?

  • Percy Jackson needs 3 recommendation letters from 3 differant Gods
  • Percy Jackson is trying to get into college thus needs 3 recommendation letters as he is told this from his father (Poseidon)

Why Should You Read It?

The plot of this book is quite good. Percy Jackson has to get 3 recommendation letters to get into college he meets 3 Gods along the way: Iris, Hebe, and Geras. He needs to find the Chalice Of The Gods which Ganymede (Cupbearer of the Gods) has sent him to retrieve when an unknown theif has stolen it. Will he find out who (Can't include TOO much spoilers)?
