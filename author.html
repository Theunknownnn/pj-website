Who Is Rick Riordan?

Rick Riordan is a famous Author, but how did he do it? His first series (Percy Jackson and the Olympians) was about greek mythology but in the modern era. Following a 12 year old called Percy Jackson who is son of Poseidon but also son of a mortal who are these people called? Demigods or Half-bloods. Monsters such as the Minotuar can smell and hunt down these "Demigods". It was a huge success in which he then made another series called : The Kane Chronicles based on Ancient Egyption Gods. He also made : The Heroes Of Olympus based around greek mythology but this time with the romans.
